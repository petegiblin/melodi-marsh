About

Melodi Marsh is a multidisciplinary artist from Camden Town, London. Known for her raw, storytelling spoken word performances, she explores themes of healing and truth alongside a career in commercial modelling. Melodi turns identity, longing, and the thresholds in between into verse made to be heard aloud—intimate, unguarded, and alive across page, stage, and screen.

Since launching her artistic journey in 2020, she has performed at notable UK venues and festivals. As a model, she has worked with global brands including Nike, Adidas, Converse, and Charlotte Tilbury, and has been featured in top-tier publications such as Notion, Wonderland, and Black Magazine (New Zealand).
